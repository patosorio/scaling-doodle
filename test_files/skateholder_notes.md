# Stakeholder Notes (Interviews)

## Owner (Talicada)
- Wants quicker proposals without losing premium feel.
- Needs visibility on margins and vendor costs.
- Prefers clear deposit and cancellation policy displayed to clients.

## Travel Designer
- Pain: chasing vendors for confirmations via WhatsApp.
- Needs templates for common routes (Marrakech → Agafay → Essaouira → Sahara).
- Wants to reuse activities and hotel blocks to save time.

## Operations
- Needs a single source of truth for vendor requests and confirmations.
- Wants reminders for pending vendor replies.
- Prefers simple status labels (Requested, Confirmed, Declined, Alternative Needed).

## Clients (Observed)
- Want clarity on what's included, pacing of days, and comfort level (hotel class).
- Prefer transparent pricing and easy next step to book (pay deposit).
