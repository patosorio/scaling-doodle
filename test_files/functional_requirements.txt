MVP Functional Requirements

1) Lead Intake
- Public form: name, email, phone, travel dates (flexible checkbox), group size, interests (culture, desert, gastronomy, shopping, wellness), budget band.
- Auto-create a lead in the CRM pipeline with stage=New.

2) CRM Pipeline
- Stages: New, Qualified, Proposal Sent, Booked, In Trip, Completed.
- Kanban view + filters by agent, date, budget band.
- Activity log: notes, attachments, timestamps.

3) Itinerary Builder
- Day-by-day structure: location, hotel, activities, transfers, meal notes.
- Price summary: per-person, total, deposit amount.
- Export: client PDF proposal (logo, brand colors, photos).

4) Vendor Coordination
- Log requests and replies (hotels, drivers, guides).
- Track costs, confirmation status, and due dates.

5) Roles & Access
- Owner: all permissions, margin view.
- Agent: manage own leads and itineraries.

6) Search
- Semantic search across leads, itineraries, vendor logs, and proposals.

7) Non-Functional
- Fast first page load for CRM users.
- Simple deployment and basic monitoring.
